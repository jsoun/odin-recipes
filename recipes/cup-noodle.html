It's claimed to be the hardest food to cook in the world!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup -- Cup Noodle
  • 1/2 cup -- Water

Directions:

  1. Grab a cup of Cup Noodle.
  2. Find & add water into the Cup.
  3. Place container in the microwave.
  4. Microwave on medium for 1min 30secs.
  5. Open the microwave door, grab some chopsticks or fork, & enjoy!
Caution!! Soup may be hot during initial consumption. Eat at your own pace!
