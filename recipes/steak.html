Ingredients:

  • 1x -- 2-inch Steak
  • 1 tbsp -- Butter
  • 1 tbsp -- Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp -- Salt
  • 1 tsp -- Pepper
  • 1x -- Rosemary or Thyme (Optional)

Directions:

  1. Coat Steak with a thin-layer of Olive Oil then season with Salt & Pepper.
  2. Heat up cast-iron skillet on the stove-top.
  3. Add butter, then place the steak on the pan.
  4. Cook one-side for about 6mins. Flip & repeat.
  5. Wait until internal temperature reaches 140F.
  6. Remove steak from heat & rest for 5 minutes before serving!
